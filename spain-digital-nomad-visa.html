Spain Digital Nomad Visa Requirements

Updated 2026 guide for remote workers, freelancers, and non-EU citizens looking to live in Spain.

What is the Spain Digital Nomad Visa?

Spain’s Digital Nomad Visa allows non-EU remote workers and freelancers to live and work legally in Spain. It combines lifestyle, tax benefits, and European stability.

Who Can Apply?

  • Remote employees for non-Spanish companies
  • Freelancers with international clients
  • Startup founders and entrepreneurs
  • IT, designers, marketers, consultants

Visa Requirements (2026)

Valid Passport: At least 1 year validity.
Minimum Monthly Income: Approx. €2,160.
Proof of Remote Work: Active contracts or agreements.
Education: University degree or 3+ years experience.

Income & Tax Benefits

  • Flat 15% income tax for up to 4 years.
  • Applies to income up to €600,000.
  • No wealth tax for most foreign assets.

Step-by-Step Application

  1. Prepare required documents (Criminal record, Insurance, Contracts).
  2. Apply at Spanish Consulate or from within Spain.
  3. Wait for approval (typically 20–45 days).
  4. Register address and obtain NIE.
  5. Apply for residence card (TIE).

Common Reasons for Rejection

  • Income falling below the required threshold.
  • Working for Spanish-based companies.
  • Incomplete or untranslated legal documents.
  • Misunderstanding complex tax rules.

Final Thoughts

Spain’s Digital Nomad Visa is an attractive European option for remote workers. Meeting income and document requirements ensures a smooth application, offering lifestyle, tax benefits, and EU access.
