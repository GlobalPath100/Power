Compare the most budget-friendly destinations for remote workers in 2026, including income requirements, visa fees, and cost of living.

What are Digital Nomad Visas?

Digital nomad visas allow remote workers to live legally in foreign countries while working online. They often have income thresholds, specific visa fees, and living cost considerations.

Top Budget-Friendly Destinations

  • Georgia – extremely low cost and easy entry
  • Mexico – flexible residency and affordable lifestyle
  • Thailand – low expenses and long-stay options
  • Portugal – affordable European option
  • Estonia – tech-friendly Europe
  • Costa Rica – relaxed lifestyle with low costs
