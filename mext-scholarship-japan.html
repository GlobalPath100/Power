Study in Japan: MEXT Scholarship 2026

A fully-funded government opportunity for global students.

Introduction to MEXT

The Monbukagakusho (MEXT) Scholarship is a highly competitive, fully funded scholarship provided by the Japanese Ministry of Education, Culture, Sports, Science, and Technology. It is designed to foster human resources who will become bridges between Japan and their home countries.

Types of MEXT Scholarships

Research Students

For Master’s and PhD applicants. Focuses heavily on your research proposal and academic excellence.

Undergraduate Students

For high school graduates. Includes 1 year of intensive Japanese language training followed by 4 years of study.

What Does the Scholarship Cover?

  • Full Tuition: 100% waiver for entrance exams, matriculation, and tuition fees.
  • Monthly Allowance: 117,000 to 145,000 JPY depending on the level of study.
  • Flights: Economy-class airfare to and from Japan at the start and end of the program.

Selection Process Steps

  1. 1
    Document Screening

    The first hurdle where your grades, motivation, and research plan are evaluated by the Japanese embassy.

  2. 2
    Written Examinations

    Tests in English and Japanese (and sometimes math/science) depending on the course.

  3. 3
    Interview

    A panel interview to assess your personality, goals, and research potential.
